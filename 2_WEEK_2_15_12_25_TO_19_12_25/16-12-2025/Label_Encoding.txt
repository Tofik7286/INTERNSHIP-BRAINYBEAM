Label Encoding: Encoding Techniques- Label Encoding & One-hot Encoder


1. Label Encoding
. Label Encoding is a technique used to convert categorical data into numerical data.
. It assigns a unique numerical value to each category present in the categorical variable.
. It is useful for ordinal categorical data, where the categories have a natural ordering, such as small, medium, large.
. Label Encoding can lead to the false assumption of a natural ordering between categories, which can affect the performance of certain machine learning algorithms.
. It is straightforward to implement and can be easily used in a variety of machine learning models.
